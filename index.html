CONTROLS

  • Press CTRL + Z to undo your last connection between 2 gates
  • Right click (or on mobile click down) to add a draggable text box to your screen that can be used to label your circuits
  • Select a logic gate by clicking the dropdown in the top left hand corner of your screen
  • Click the "Add Node" button to add your logic gate to the editor and click and drag on the gate to move it around
  • To connect gates, simply click and drag on the blue circle of one gate and drop the red circle onto another gate and a wire will be added between them
  • Use an input gate by clicking on it to toggle it on and off
  • Output gates can show you whether certain parts of your circuit are on or off, they can be used to represent a 0 or 1
  • You can switch between light and dark mode by clicking the button with a moon or sun on it
